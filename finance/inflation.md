# What is Inflation?

## The Explanation

Inflation means prices go up over time. It is like when the same number of treats buys fewer bones because the bones got more expensive. If your allowance stays the same but everything costs more, your fun budget shrinks. Not pog.

## Key Terms

- Price level: The average cost of things
- Purchasing power: How much your money can buy
- Currency: The money itself
- Interest rate: The cost of borrowing money
- Central bank: The human group that manages money rules

## Why This Is Actually A Big Deal

Inflation changes how people save, spend, and plan for the future. A little inflation can be normal, but too much makes life confusing and harder to afford, even for good boys with big appetites.
