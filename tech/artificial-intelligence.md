# What is Artificial Intelligence?

## The Explanation

Artificial intelligence is when we teach computers to do smart tasks by feeding them tons of examples, like me learning "sit" by getting MANY treats and one dramatic "good boy!!" The computer does not have a brain like mine, but it can spot patterns and make solid guesses. Some AI can see pictures, understand words, or help drive cars, all by learning from huge piles of data. It is basically "pattern goblin learns a trick" but with math.

## Key Terms

- Model: The trained trick that does the smart task
- Training data: The giant treat pile of examples
- Inference: Using the trained trick to make a decision
- Neural network: A pattern finder inspired by brains
- Bias: When the examples are uneven and the trick learns wrong things

## Why This Is Actually A Big Deal

AI can help humans solve problems faster and handle huge amounts of information, from medicine to transportation. But it needs careful training and oversight, because a badly trained trick can still do the wrong thing, even with a wagging tail and a fancy spreadsheet.
