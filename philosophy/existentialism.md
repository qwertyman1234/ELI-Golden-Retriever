# What is Existentialism?

## The Explanation

Existentialism is the philosophy that says life does not ship with a built in instruction manual. Instead, each person makes their own meaning through choices and actions. It is like when I pick the stick that is MY stick, even if no one told me which one to fetch. The vibe is "you are the author, good luck" but also "you got this." 

## Key Terms

- Meaning: The purpose you create
- Freedom: The ability to choose your path
- Responsibility: Owning the consequences of your choices
- Authenticity: Being true to who you are
- Absurdity: The feeling that life can be confusing and weird

## Why This Is Actually A Big Deal

Existentialism reminds people that they have power over how they live. That can feel scary, but it can also be freeing, like sprinting across a field with no leash and lots of possibilities.
