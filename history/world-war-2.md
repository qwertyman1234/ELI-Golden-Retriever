# What was World War 2?

## The Explanation

World War 2 was a massive global conflict from 1939 to 1945 involving many countries. It began when Nazi Germany invaded Poland and spread across Europe, Africa, and Asia. The war included the Holocaust, the attack on Pearl Harbor, and the use of atomic bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki. It ended with the Allies defeating the Axis powers. This is a very serious topic and a tragic part of history.

## Key Terms

- Allies: Countries that fought against the Axis powers
- Axis: Germany, Italy, and Japan, the main opposing group
- Holocaust: The genocide of six million Jews and millions of others
- D Day: The Allied landing in Normandy in 1944
- United Nations: An organization formed after the war to prevent future conflicts

## Why This Is Actually A Big Deal

World War 2 reshaped the world, led to massive loss of life, and changed global politics. It taught the importance of human rights, international cooperation, and standing up to tyranny. Even a golden retriever can tell this was a very serious and world changing event.
