# What is Gravity?

## The Explanation

Gravity is the invisible "yoink" that pulls stuff together and keeps planets from yeeting themselves into space. Big things like Earth have lots of mass, so they pull on smaller things. That is why I return to the floor after a max height treat jump, even though my heart says "hover mode"!!

## Key Terms

- Mass: How much "stuff" a thing has, like a very chonky bone
- Attraction: The pull between objects
- Orbit: A steady loop around a bigger object
- Weight: How hard gravity pulls on you
- Force: A push or pull that changes motion

## Why This Is Actually A Big Deal

Gravity shapes the entire universe. It makes stars, keeps planets in orbits, and keeps our paws on the ground. Without it there are no walks, no fetch, and no home base at all!!
